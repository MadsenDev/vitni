# Relationships

Relationships connect two nodes with a typed, directional edge. Most have subtypes for precision.

## Categories

**Associated With** — Generic link when no specific type fits. Subtypes: linked to, alias of, same as, possible same as.

**Family / Personal** — Person to person connections. Subtypes: parent of, child of, sibling of, spouse of, friend of, neighbor of.

**Employment / Affiliation** — Person to organization. Subtypes: employee of, contractor for, member of, affiliate of, founder of, executive of, student at, patient at.

**Ownership / Control** — Who controls what. Subtypes: owns, leases, controls, administers, assigned to, registered to.

**Located At** — Physical or hosted location. Subtypes: resides at, based at, headquartered at, operates at, hosted at, stored at.

**Communicated With** — Contact between accounts, phones, or people. Subtypes: called, texted, emailed, messaged, met with, video called.

**Participated In** — Who was at an event or incident. Subtypes: attended, organized, spoke at, present at, departed from, arrived at.

**Documented By** — Links entities to evidence. Subtypes: mentioned in, cited in, authored, sent, received, recorded in, captured in.

**Used / Accessed** — Digital access and usage. Subtypes: used, logged into, accessed, visited, operated, connected to.

**Financially Linked** — Money flows. Subtypes: paid, received from, transferred to, funded, donated to, purchased from.

**Case / Incident Role** — Roles in formal investigations. Subtypes: suspect in, victim in, witness in, investigator on, reported by, evidence for.

**Threat / Harm** — Harmful actions. Subtypes: threatened, attacked, defrauded, extorted, harassed.

## Properties

Every relationship can have:
- **Date** — when the connection occurred or was observed
- **Confidence** — Verified, Asserted, or Unverified
